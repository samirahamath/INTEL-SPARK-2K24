Three Amazing Venues for events

The Symposium is where students can unleash their creativity and technical skills. Participants will work in teams to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems, competing against other brilliant minds from different colleges. The event promises exciting challenges and rewards for the most impactful projects.


The Debate Competition invites students to engage in intellectual duels, testing their oratory skills and critical thinking. Participants will argue on various pressing topics, defending their stance with logic and eloquence. This event is an excellent platform for those looking to refine their public speaking abilities and learn the art of persuasion.
